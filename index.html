Secret Menu Scotland

Serena needed strong use of imagery and a premium feel to convey the high-end expectations shes wanted to communicate to potential clients. Although overall it is a simple site, she wanted to make sure it had a big impact.

Cellexus

Cellexus provided strong branding guides, which meant tailor-making this website was a breeze. Cellexus needed to include important techinical and scientific information, without sacrificing the friendly face of their company.

Cool2Talk

NHS Tayside needed to update their (very) old online-anonymous-question-box for young people. Rather than custom-building an expensive web-app, we opted to integrate with existing ticketing software, Freshdesk.

The website provides an interface for councelors to answer dozens of weekly questions, live-chat, write help articles, and generally provide a safe-space for young people in Tayside.

Oralift

Oralift required an customer facing eCommerce site to go alongside their mobile app and web-app admin site.

This bright website markets the company and allows users to purchase their main product and book support appointments.

Cypex

The guys at Cypex provide very complex scientific products. I created a website that lets them catalogue hundreds of Bactosomes, Enzymes and instructional documents for potential customers to browse and for existing customers to find information.
